Functional Location

This represents a physical or logical location used to locate the Technical Objects on which the maintenance processes are based. These can be structured by dimensions such as Function (purpose), Process, Technology, or Spatial/location. The intial setup is flexible according to the above criteria, but the structure is typically permanent and expected to be retained for the lifetime of the plant or enterprise.
